A personal project about a very special musician I discovered a few years ago.

In 2019, I watched a movie that blew my mind. It was about a japanese guy called Akio Sakurai, who became obsessed with the legendary guitar player from Led Zeppelin, Jimmy Page, to the point where he literally started dressing up like him. The resemblance was so powerful, he became known as Mr Jimmy.

Visit site Jimmy Sakurai

The idea

Create a painfully accurate representation of his obsession by developing a website you can actually play, just like a guitar.

The assets

Every image in this project was a mix of DALL-E and Photoshop.

Amazing fact: Akio currently tours around the world with Jason Bonham, Led Zeppelin's original drummer's son.
