A minimalistic, practical website for a vinyl store.

For my second semester final project, my team and I worked with a danish shop that is a vinyl store, a cafe, and a live music bar. Much of their revenue comes from selling records online. The thing is... their website wasn't working.

Visit site Jazzcup

The idea

Create a clean, modern website so JazzCup's customers can quickly buy records and see the calendar for live events.

Putting into practice

This project allowed me to work with fetching and storing data, creating databases and methods like forEach.
