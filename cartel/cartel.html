A 3d website for an immersive user experience.

This danish brand turns plastic waste into bikinis. The question was: how do we make the younger generations care about the plastic contaminating the water?

Visit site Copenhagen Cartel

The set-up

First, my team and I had to come up with a multimedia campaing for Copenhagen Cartel. Second, we had to figure out how to show it. That's when I realized three.js would be great.

Assets

Most of the images in this project come from Unsplash. The bikini items, logos and fonts were provided by the brand. Technology-wise, I used Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, three.js, Vite and JavaScript.
